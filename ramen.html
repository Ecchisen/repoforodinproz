Ramen Noodles

This soup is just extremly good......You can find ramen noodles at most grocery stores and supermarkets, or at Asain food shop/stores.

Ingredients

  • 3.5 cups of vegetable broth
  • 1 or 2 package of ramen noodles with dried veggies
  • 2 teaspoons of soy sauce
  • one-half teaspoon of chili oil
  • one-half teaspoon of minced fresh ginger root
  • 1 teaspoon of sesame oil
  • 2 green onions, sliced

Directions

  1. In a medium saucepan combine broth and noodles.
  2. Cover and bring to a boil over high heat; stir to break up noodles.
  3. Reduce heat to medium and add soy sauce, chili oil and ginger.
  4. Simmer, uncovered, for 10 minutes.
  5. Stir in sesame oil and garnish with green onions.
