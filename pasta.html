Pasta~~

Pasta is easy to make and full of flavour. It can take almost around 15 minutes to prepare. It tastes great hot or cold.

Ingredients

  • 1 (15 ounce) package pasta
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1/2 cup of chopped onions
  • 2 and a half tablespoons pesto
  • salt to taste
  • ground black pepper to taste
  • 2 tablespoons of grated Parmesan cheese

Directions

  1. Fill a large pot with lightly salted water and bring to a rolling boil. Stir in pasta and return to a boil.
  2. Cook pasta uncovered, stirring occasionally, until tender yet firm to the bite, about 8 to 10 minutes. Drain and transfer into a large bowl.
  3. Meanwhile, heat oil in a frying pan over medium-low heat. Add onion; cook and stir until softened, about 3 minutes. Stir in pesto, salt, and pepper until warmed through.
  4. Add pesto mixture to hot pasta; stir in grated cheese and toss well to coat
